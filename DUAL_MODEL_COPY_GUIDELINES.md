## âœ… Checklist

Before publishing copy, ask:

- [ ] Are both paths presented equally?
- [ ] Is paid framed positively (not as "skip")?
- [ ] Are trade-offs clear for both?
- [ ] Can users easily compare?

## ğŸ¯ Success Metrics

**Good Balance =**
- 40-60% choose free path
- 40-60% choose paid path
- Low confusion rate
- High satisfaction with chosen path
- Low path-switching (fewer users changing mind)

**Bad Balance =**
- 90%+ choose one path (skewed)
- High confusion/comparison time
- Low satisfaction ("I didn't know I could...")
- High path-switching

---

**Remember:** The goal is to maximize TOTAL conversions (free + paid), not push users to one option.
